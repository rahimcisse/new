Frequently Asked Questions

iConnect is a professional networking platform that bridges the gap between talented job seekers and forward-thinking employers. Our platform uses advanced matching algorithms to connect the right people with the right opportunities.

Job seekers can use iConnect completely free. Employers have access to basic features for free, with premium plans available for advanced features like priority listings and detailed analytics.

Simply click the "Sign Up" button on our homepage, choose whether you're an employer or job seeker, and follow the guided setup process. You'll be up and running in under 5 minutes.

After logging in, click "Post a Job" in your dashboard. Fill out the job details, requirements, and company information. Your listing will be live within minutes of submission.

Applications are automatically organized in your employer dashboard. You can filter by qualifications, experience level, and other criteria to find the best matches quickly.

Premium plans include priority job placement, advanced candidate filtering, detailed analytics, unlimited job postings, and dedicated account support.

Complete all profile sections, add relevant skills and keywords, upload a professional photo, and regularly update your experience. Our system will suggest improvements based on industry standards.

Your "My Applications" dashboard shows all submitted applications, their current status, and any messages from employers. You'll also receive email notifications for status updates.
